About

The Project

I Cried Here aims to normalise emotions by making them visible in the spaces we exist in.

Crying happens everyday, everywhere. At the same time, crying is usually hidden - because it is a private affair, because we need to feel safe to let it out, because of shame... But when crying is so hidden, it can be easy to believe we are the only ones doing it, easy to blame ourselves for a perceived over-emotionality.

I created I Cried Here to explore different ways of leaving traces of these emotions, which are really traces of our humanity. Hoping that next time you cry, seeing a sticker "I cried here" can make you feel connected to the big siblinghood of humans who cry.
